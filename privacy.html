Privacy Policy

The short version: we don't collect your data and your files never leave your device.

Last updated: April 2026

TL;DR: Palettly runs entirely in your browser. We don't store your files, we don't require an account, and we don't sell your data. The only data we collect is anonymous analytics through Google Analytics to understand how people use the site.

Who we are

Palettly is a product of Trioni, a web development and apps studio. When we say "we", "us" or "our", we mean Trioni.

What data we collect

Files and content

None. All tools on Palettly run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. When you upload an image, PDF or any other file, it is processed locally on your device and never transmitted to any server. We have no access to any files you use with our tools.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage data. This includes pages visited, time on site, general geographic region (country level), browser type and device type. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.

Donations

If you choose to support us via Ko-fi or PayPal, those transactions are handled entirely by Ko-fi and PayPal respectively. We do not store your payment information.

Local storage

Some tools save your preferences (like selected language) in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and you can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics cookies — anonymous usage tracking (_ga, _gid)
  • Google AdSense cookies — ad personalization
  • localStorage — language preference, tool settings (not technically a cookie but similar)

We do not use cookies for login, tracking or profiling individual users.

Third-party services

Palettly uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous analytics
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • Google Fonts — web fonts (font names loaded from Google servers)
  • Ko-fi — optional donations
  • PayPal — optional donations
  • Vercel — hosting (serves HTML/CSS/JS files only)
  • jsDelivr / cdnjs — open source library CDNs

Your rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to request deletion of. If you have any concerns about data collected by Google Analytics or AdSense, you can manage those preferences through Google's own tools.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at our contact page.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
